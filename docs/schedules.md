# Access Schedules

Current Russian draft:

- [Расписания доступа](schedules.ru.md)

Schedules should let parents select devices, weekdays, time ranges, and block/allow actions. They apply only to devices outside the allowlist and blocklist. Temporary access quick buttons include +15 minutes, +30 minutes, +1 hour, +2 hours, +3 hours, +5 hours, until end of day, and until bedtime.
